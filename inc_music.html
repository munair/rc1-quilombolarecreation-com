Berimbau Class

Join us weekdays at 9:30 PM for Berimbau Class. We will work various popular toques and sing Capoeira songs during this period. Class is 50 minutes long.

Pandeiro Class

Join us weekdays at 9:30 PM for Pandeiro Class. We will accompany the berimbau and sing Capoeira songs during this period. Class is 50 minutes long.

Atabaque Class

Join us weekdays at 10:30 PM (by RSVP only) for Atabaque Class. We will work Samba, Barravento, or Congo in these sessions. Class is 50 minutes long.
